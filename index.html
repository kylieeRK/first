Beef with celery

  • 1/2 lb rump steak
  • 1/4 lb regular celery
  • 2 ozs leeks or scallions
  • 1 small slice peeled fresh ginger
  • 3 tbs sesame seed oil
  • 1-2 fresh hot chilies,seeded and julienned
  • 2 tbs black bean sauce
  • tsp sugar wine
  • 1 tsp rice vinegar

    • Steps

    1. cut the beef into shreds the size of match sticks.cut the celery and leeks or scallions into julienne the same size as the beef.Cut the ginger into julienne
    2. gently heat the sesame oil in a preheated wok,add the meat,and stir-fry for about 1 minute.Add the ginger,chilies,and plack bean sauce,and mix well
    3. add the celery and leeks or scallions and stir-fry for 2 minutes.Add the sugar rice wine,and vinegar,and stir-fry for another minute.serve hot
